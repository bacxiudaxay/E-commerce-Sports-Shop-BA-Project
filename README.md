## 🚨 Business Problem

Traditional sports shops face operational challenges such as:
- **Manual Order Management:** Orders are handled through Facebook, Zalo, and Excel, leading to inefficient tracking.
- **Inventory Inconsistency:** Lack of a centralized database results in incorrect pricing and stock information.
- **Limited Online Experience:** Customers cannot browse products anytime and must message the shop for basic details.

## ⚖️ Key Business Rules

- **BR-06:** Cart will be cleared automatically after an order is successfully created.
- **BR-08:** An order cannot be cancelled once its status is "Shipped".
- **BR-13:** Admin cannot see customer's password in plain text (Security Standard).
